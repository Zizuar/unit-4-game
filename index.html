Welcome to the Kitty Death Star Attack Game~ In this game you will be presented with 4 different attack craft. Your objective is to plan an attack pattern that matches the power estimate to destroy the death star. For each click of the ship you are adding one of that ship to your attack pattern. The catch? You don't know how much each ship adds to your attack score until you click on it! You must match the death star score number in order to overpower it's defenses and destroy it. Fall under the number and your squadron will be destroied. Go over the number and your squadron will get caught in the blast and destroied. Good luck Commander!
